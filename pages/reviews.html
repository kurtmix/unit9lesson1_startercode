Anime for You!

These Anime are generally good for most age groups to enjoy

Attack On Titan: This anime is a horror, action, mystery awesome sause of a show that is centered around the idea of titans (huge monsters) that backed humanity in a corner and now are among humans, this anime is one of my top 3 because its just soooo good.

Full Metal Alchemist: This anime is more of a fantsy, magic, drama and action packed show that's centered around the idea of "equivalent exchange" where the main protagonist Edward Elric becomes a alchemist and discovers it through a dark truth.

ToraDora: Romance, heartbreak and all the feels you can ever feel in a anime, thats my sum of it. Its a really good anime because it makes the viewer gets emotionally attatched to the characters as their highschool life plays out.
